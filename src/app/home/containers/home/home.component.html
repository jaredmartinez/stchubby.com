Dear St. Chubby friends,

After careful consideration we have made the decision to postpone all upcoming and in-person events for the 2020 year due to the COVID-19 epidemic.  Unfortunately this includes our scheduled fundraising golf tournament in April.  Our first and foremost priority is for your health and well-being. This includes families, friends, households, and colleagues. In light of current and rapidly changing developments surrounding the spread of COVID-19,  we believe that rescheduling these events is the best way we can help ensure everyone’s safety, deliver the quality event you expect and support our charity.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your patience as we navigate these unexpected circumstances.

We thank you for your continued support of St. Chubby during these unprecedented times. We wish you and your families good health.

﻿ All the best,
Bill May & the St. Chubby Board of Directors
